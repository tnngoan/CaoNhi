HISTORICAL RESEARCH ARCHIVE REQUIREMENT

The platform must launch with a **historical research archive of at least 150 professional investment articles** covering the past **3 years of Vietnam stock market activity**.

These articles must **NOT be randomly generated**.

Instead, each article must be **based on real historical market conditions that existed at the time of publication.**

The goal is to simulate a broker who has been **publishing disciplined weekly research for years.**

---

REAL MARKET RESEARCH REQUIREMENT

For each article:

1. Identify the **actual market conditions during that week** in Vietnam.

Research should reference:

VNIndex movement
Major macroeconomic events
Interest rate changes
Foreign investor flows
Sector performance
Major Vietnamese company news
Government policy affecting markets

Each article must reflect the **real investor sentiment and macro context that existed at that time**.

Example:

If VNIndex declined sharply in a given week, the article should discuss:

Market correction
Risk sentiment
Capital outflows
Sector weakness

If the market rallied, the article should reference:

Liquidity increase
Sector leadership
Economic optimism

Articles must feel like they were **written during that specific week in history**.

---

ARTICLE TIMELINE GENERATION

Generate articles starting from:

Last week → going backwards up to 3 years ago.

Publishing cadence:

Monday — Vietnam Market Outlook
Wednesday — Stock Pick
Friday — Portfolio Strategy

Monthly — Sector Report

This creates a realistic archive of **150+ posts**.

---

DATA SOURCES TO REFERENCE

When generating historical context, research and reference:

Vietnam stock market activity
VNIndex performance
Major Vietnamese listed companies
Macroeconomic conditions in Vietnam
Global events impacting emerging markets

The writing should sound like a **professional investment research note reacting to current events of that week**.

---

ARTICLE QUALITY

Each article must include:

Title
Publish date
Summary
Tags
Sector references
Relevant stock tickers

Content structure:

Market context
Analysis of conditions
Investment interpretation
Portfolio implications
Risks

Articles should be **800–1200 words**.

---

DATABASE STORAGE

All articles must be stored in a database so they can dynamically power the website.

Use a schema like:

articles

id
title
slug
category
publish_date
summary
content
tags
related_stocks
created_at

The website should automatically:

Sort articles by publish date
Create category pages
Display archives by month and year

---

ARCHIVE PURPOSE

The historical archive establishes **immediate credibility** when the platform launches.

Visitors should see:

• years of research history
• disciplined publishing cadence
• consistent market analysis

This should resemble the archives of professional financial research platforms such as Bloomberg or Seeking Alpha.

However, the archive must reflect **real historical market context**, not generic content.
